Pok-pok Bawel

I designed this logo for my mother’s and aunt’s new business. They plan to make a cafe in Bandung, Indonesia. The name of the business Pok-Pok Bawel. Pok-pok is the sound that chicken makes in my native language and bawel is the act of being annoyingly talkative. In the logo, I use bold colours such as red and yellow to make logo stands out more.

Seikou

I design this logo for my friend’s business project that he had in his school.
He plans on selling cinnamon and apple flavoured danishes.
Hence, I decided to incorporate the main ingredient which is apple and cinnamon
in this logo.

The logo name “Seiko” comes from a Japanese language which means success
and the colours that I use are colours that goes well with
the natural shade of cinnamon and apple.

Larfa In Vitro

This is also one of the logo that I made with my best friend to apply for a competition. The winner’s logo will be picked as the main logo for my school’s science fair. The theme behind the event is innovation through compassion. Hence I decided, to make the logo in a form of a rocket.
