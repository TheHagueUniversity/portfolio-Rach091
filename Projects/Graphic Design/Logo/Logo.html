Pok-pok Bawel
I designed this logo for my mother’s and aunt’s new business. They plan to make a cafe in Bandung, Indonesia. The name of the business Pok-Pok Bawel. Pok-pok is the sound that chicken makes in my native language and bawel is the act of being annoyingly talkative. In the logo, I use bold colours such as red and yellow to make logo stands out more.
Seikou
I design this logo for my friend’s business project that he had in his school. He plans on selling cinnamon and apple flavoured danishes. Hence, I decided to incorporate the main ingredient which is apple and cinnamon in this logo. The logo name “Seiko” comes from a Japanese language which means success and the colours that I use are colours that goes well with the natural shade of cinnamon and apple.
