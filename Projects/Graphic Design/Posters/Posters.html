Civics Poster

I designed this poster for my school’s civics assignment. During that day,
my country is celebrating Youth Pledge Day, which is a declaration made on 28 October 1928
by young Indonesian nationalists in the Second Youth Congress.
Hence, I decided to make the poster in a form of an old newspaper,
featuring one of the most well known patriot. Since it’s in a form of an old newspaper,
most of the colours are in the shade of brown and black, to make it look vintage.
